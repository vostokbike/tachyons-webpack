Hub compatibility

The chain line can be adjusted from 46 mm to 56 mm by rotating the jockey wheel axle nuts. For Shimano Internal Hubs, use 7L/R black/grey anti-rotation washers for proper cable routing: there is a recess in the chain tensioner to accomodate the anti-rotation washer. For Rohloff, this recess can be ignored completely.

Sprocket Clearance

Our chain tensioner's arms are longer then of a stock Brompton one. Thus, it will clear all stock Alfine/Nexus sprockets and Rohloff sprockets up to 18T.

Fitting

An axle nut or a quick release skewer can be used to secure the tensioner on the dropout. The tensioner was designed for our custom triangle, but can also be fitted to a standard Brompton dropout.

Vostok Derailleur Hanger

If you would like to assemble an ultralight cassette Brompton, get our derailleur hanger. It will interface perfectly with the Vostok Triangle.
