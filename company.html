Company

Vostok is a Russian brand and a manufacturer of titanium bicycle components. Our production plant is based in Korolev, Moscow Region and our web store operates out of Moscow. The brand was established in 2016 with a mission to produce aftermarket Brompton components and accessories. Production of titanium bicycle frames and parts in Korolev, the birthplace and home of the Russian space industry, has been live since 1991.
